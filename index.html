Three libraries.
One philosophy.

Each library solves a narrow, painful, measurable problem. Closed-source — but we publish the benchmarks, the methodology, and a demo binary so you can verify before you license. No vendor magic.

Stable

kvopt

KV-cache optimizer for transformer inference. Adaptive eviction, fused dequant kernels, and a paging layer that turns long-context inference from impossible to interactive.

vs. vLLM 0.6 · Llama 70B +3.4× tok/s
Beta

qsim-rl

State-vector quantum simulator with a tensor-network fallback. Built for hybrid quantum-classical workloads where you actually need both halves to be fast.

vs. Qiskit Aer · 30q GHZ +2.1× wall-time
Alpha

nnkern

Hand-tuned attention & MoE routing kernels for sub-batch inference. Currently shipping for Hopper, Blackwell, and MI300; ARM Neoverse in testing.

vs. FlashAttn-3 · head 128 +18% throughput
