A note on scale.

Raya Labs is one engineer. That sounds like a weakness — it's the whole point. No PM overhead, no roadmap politics, no "shipped to look busy" releases. Every line is written, benchmarked, and supported by the same person.

01

Closed-source, by design.

Each library represents years of compounding optimization work. Closed-source isn't gatekeeping — it's how a one-person lab funds the next benchmark suite.

02

Open numbers.

The code is closed, but the proof isn't. Methodology, raw JSON logs, and reproduction scripts are available for every published claim. Losses disclosed alongside wins.

03

Talk to the builder.

No sales funnel. No SDR. When you email, you get the person who wrote the kernels. Licensing terms are scoped per-customer — just ask.
